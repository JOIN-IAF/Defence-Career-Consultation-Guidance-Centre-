Eligibility for Air Force X & Y Group (Agniveer Vayu) 2026-27

Common Requirements (Both Groups):

  • Age: 17.5 to 21 years (confirm in latest notification)
  • Gender: Unmarried Male / Female candidates
  • Physical Standards: Minimum height 152.5 cm, good medical fitness

Educational Qualification:

  • X Group (Technical): 12th with Physics, Maths & English (50% aggregate + 50% in English) OR 3-year Diploma
  • Y Group (Non-Technical): 12th in Any Stream with 50% aggregate + 50% in English

Note: Always check the official notification on agnipathvayu.cdac.in or careerairforce.gov.in.

Selection Process (Brief)

1. Online Written Exam (X: Physics, Maths, English | Y: English, Reasoning, GA)
2. Physical Fitness Test (1.6 km run in \~6:30 min, etc.)
3. Adaptability Tests
4. Medical Examination

We provide complete guidance for both X Group and Y Group.
